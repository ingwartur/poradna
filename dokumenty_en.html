Documents for Stay in Slovakia

1. Temporary Protection

  • Available for Ukrainian citizens and family members who arrived after February 24, 2022.
  • Apply at the Foreign Police departments.
  • Required: passport, photo, proof of accommodation.
  • Issued for one year with possibility of extension.

2. Residence Permit

  • For work, study, or family reunification.
  • Application must be submitted in person at the police.
  • Documents: justification (contract, university letter), photo, insurance, housing proof, financial resources.

3. Identification Documents

  • International passport – main document for travel and identification.
  • Ukrainian domestic passport or ID card.
  • Birth certificate (for children).

4. Housing Registration

  • Required for certain services and assistance.
  • Application at the local municipal office (Mestský úrad).
  • Required: rental agreement or confirmation from the property owner.

5. Social Cards and Health Insurance

  • After protection is granted, you receive the Slovak health card.
  • It may be required at the doctor’s, bank, etc.

Tips

  • Always keep copies of all documents.
  • Scan important papers to PDF or store them in the cloud.
  • Track document expiration dates and renew them in time.
