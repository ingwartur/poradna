Housing in Slovakia: search, rental, registration

1. Searching for housing

  • Popular websites: nehnutelnosti.sk, bazos.sk, reality.sk
  • Facebook groups for Ukrainians in Slovakia often contain current offers.
  • Common options: apartments, shared rooms, dormitories.
  • Pay attention to location, utilities, and conditions.

2. Renting a home

  • The rental contract should be in writing and include: parties, object of rent, price, term, cancellation terms.
  • A deposit is usually required (typically one month upfront).
  • The tenant must maintain the property; the landlord must ensure suitable conditions.

3. Housing registration

  • Required for legal stay, residence permit, or temporary protection.
  • Registration is done at the local municipal office (Mestský úrad).
  • Required: lease agreement or landlord's written consent, passport or residence permit.

4. Living with host families

  • At the beginning of the war, state support programs for host families were active.
  • Both parties had rights and obligations—registered with the police.
  • Accommodation can later turn into a standard rental contract.

5. Common issues and tips

  • If refused a contract, request a written explanation.
  • Avoid paying “in cash without receipts.”
  • Verify whether the landlord is legally authorized to rent the property.
  • Keep copies of the lease and payment confirmations.
